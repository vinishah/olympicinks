Basic Information

There exist two types of inks for printing by flexographic and rotogravure:

  • Liquid inks
  • Metal decoration inks and coatings and varnish

All colour substrates are printed on:

  • Polyethylene
  • BOPP
  • Polyester
  • Tin Sheets

What We Produce

Types of Inks Uses
Olam Gr. BOPP Inks BOPP lamination
(Where BOPP treatment should not be less than 38-42 dynes)
Olyface Gr. BOPP Inks High H.R. surface printing jobs on BOPP
(Where BOPP treatment should not be less than 38-42 dynes)
Olam Gr. Polyester Inks Polyester lamination
(Recommended only for chemically coated polyester)
Olyfilm Gr. PVC Inks Surface printing on PVC
(Shrink films)
Concentrated Inks BOPP lamination and polyester lamination
Flexo/ Gravure Water Resistant Inks Surface printing on non heat resistant products

Flexo Water based Inks Printing on cartons

OIL Polyspeed Inks Surface printing on plastic container and in screen painting
